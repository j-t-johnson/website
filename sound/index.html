Harbor Voices

Harbor Voices is a social art collective conceptualized by Stephanie Benenson. Featuring the real spoken-word stories of family and immigrant histories settling in the greater Boston area, combined with sounds of the sea and city, with compelling laser visuals.

Harbor Voices has had installations shown in Gloucester, MA, The Armory in New York City, Illuminus Boston 2018, and most recently the House of Blues Boston in collaboration with Big Brothers Big Sisters of Massachusetts Bay.

For more info, visit harborvoices.com
